Dance & Music




‘It is well said that music is the food of soul’. Keeping the same view in mind, a special music
room has been designed to give proper training of music to the students. Students are given
vocal as well as instrumental knowledge. It is well equipped with modern instruments like casios
, guitars, keyboards, woofer system etc. Our co-curricular activities adify the students with learning
in discipline, fitness, team spirit and confidence which help to groom their overall personality.

Dance is an expression & untold happiness and in India it is linked to mythology and culture as
well. So, in order to make the students aware of different dance forms of India as well as other
countries, dance rooms have been made which are fully equipped with latest technology and
modern equipments. Students learn Indian as well as other forms of dance so that they can
cope up with the changing trends. Moreover, nowadays dance has become a passion for
everyone. So, to fulfill today’s demands, students are given chances to explore their hidden
ability through dance.
